Xura - Zero-Friction Thoughts

A great idea has a short half-life. By the time you unlock your phone, find the right app, and start typing, it is already fading. Xura exists to close that gap.

Speak a thought. Pocket your phone. Xura does the rest.

How it works

  1. Tap to capture. Open Xura from the home screen widget, the Action Button, Siri, or the app icon. Recording starts immediately — no menus, no loading screens.
  2. Say your thought. Speak naturally. Xura listens, transcribes, and classifies your words entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to a server.
  3. Route with one tap. A notification appears with a suggested destination — a reminder, a note, a message. Tap Send from the lock screen and you are done.

The whole flow — from tap to filed — is designed to take under three seconds.

Your words stay on your device

Every other app in this space sends your audio or transcript to a cloud server for processing. Xura does not. Transcription and classification happen entirely on-device using Apple's built-in speech recognition and a trained on-device model.

A thought only leaves your device when you explicitly choose to send it to an integration. That decision is always yours. See our Privacy Policy for the full picture.

Routes thoughts to the tools you already use

Xura is not a notes app or a task manager. It is a fast intake valve that files your thoughts into the apps you rely on every day.

  • Tasks go to Apple Reminders, Things 3, or Todoist.
  • Notes go to Apple Notes or Obsidian.
  • Messages open a pre-filled Apple Messages compose sheet.

Apple Reminders, Apple Notes, and Apple Messages are free. Things 3, Obsidian, and Todoist are available with Xura Pro. Learn more on the Features page.

Nothing is ever lost

If you dismiss a notification or simply do not act on it, Xura saves the thought to an inbox inside the app. Every capture is kept until you decide what to do with it.
