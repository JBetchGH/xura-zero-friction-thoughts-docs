Features

Everything Xura does, in plain terms

Xura has one job: get a thought out of your head and into the right place before you lose it. Every feature on this page serves that goal.

Instant voice capture

Tap the widget, the Action Button, or the app icon and Xura opens directly to the capture screen. There is no home screen, no menu, no form to fill. Recording starts immediately.

Speak your thought. Xura detects when you have stopped talking and ends the recording automatically after 2.5 seconds of silence. You can also tap the screen to stop early. The whole capture takes under 3 seconds.

A waveform animation shows the app is listening. After a couple of seconds a subtle "Tap to stop" hint appears. That is the entire capture interface.

On-device transcription and classification

The moment you stop speaking, Xura transcribes your words and figures out what kind of thought it is, all on your device. Nothing is sent to a server. No audio, no transcript, nothing.

Xura sorts every capture into one of three categories:

  • Task - something you need to do ("remind me to renew car insurance before Friday")
  • Note - an idea, observation, or piece of information ("the interesting thing about distributed systems is that failure is the default")
  • Message - something addressed to a person ("tell James the kickoff is moved to Thursday at 3pm")

Transcription completes in under half a second. Classification adds another fraction of a second on top. By the time you have pocketed your phone, the work is done.

See Privacy for a full explanation of how your data stays on your device.

Smart routing via notifications

After classification, Xura sends you a notification with the thought and a suggested destination. You do not need to open the app. You can act from the lock screen.

Every notification has three options:

  • Send - routes the thought to the suggested integration immediately
  • Change - opens a simple picker so you can choose a different destination
  • Save to Inbox - holds the thought in Xura until you are ready to deal with it

If you swipe the notification away, the thought is saved to the inbox automatically. Nothing is ever lost.

Integrations

Xura routes thoughts to the apps you already use. You choose one preferred destination per category (task, note, message) during setup. You can change your preferences at any time in Settings.

Free integrations

These are included for every user at no cost:

  • Apple Reminders - for tasks
  • Apple Notes - for notes
  • Apple Messages - for messages (opens a pre-filled compose sheet with the recipient and your words ready to send)

Pro integrations

A Xura Pro subscription unlocks routing to third-party apps:

  • Things 3 - for tasks
  • Obsidian - for notes (you set your vault name in Settings)
  • Todoist - for tasks (connects via a secure sign-in from Settings)

These apps must be installed on your device. If a Pro integration app is not installed, Xura falls back to your free integration for that category without losing your thought.

The inbox

The inbox is a safety net, not a primary feature. Any thought you do not route right away, whether you tapped "Save to Inbox", dismissed the notification, or captured a burst of thoughts before bed, is waiting in Settings under Inbox.

From the inbox you can route each thought to any of your configured integrations with a single tap. Nothing expires and nothing is deleted automatically.

Widget and Action Button support

Xura offers two widget sizes to put capture one tap away from anywhere:

  • Lock screen widget - a small circular button on your lock screen
  • Home screen widget - a small tile that also shows your most recent capture

On iPhone 15 Pro and later, you can assign Xura to the Action Button. One press starts recording instantly, even when the phone is locked.

You can also ask Siri to start a capture by saying "Xura", "Capture a thought", or "Add a Xura".

Xura Pro

Xura is free to use with Apple Reminders, Notes, and Messages. Pro unlocks Things 3, Obsidian, and Todoist routing, plus a higher monthly capture allowance.

Pro is available as a subscription:

  • Monthly - £1.00 per month
  • Annual - £10.00 per year (equivalent to two months free)

You can subscribe from the paywall that appears when you tap a Pro integration in Settings. Existing purchases can be restored from the same screen. Xura never interrupts a capture to prompt an upgrade.

Free users can route up to 25 thoughts per month. When you reach that limit, Xura offers the option to upgrade or save the thought to your inbox instead.
