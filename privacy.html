Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Xura - Zero-Friction Thoughts collects about you, how that information is used, and the choices you have. It applies to the Xura - Zero-Friction Thoughts app and related services.

Effective date: 2025-07-16

Information we collect

The table below describes each category of information we collect, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and any third parties we share it with.

Voice audio (microphone input)

To capture spoken thoughts for transcription. Audio is processed entirely on-device and is not stored after transcription completes.

  • Retention: transient — not stored.
Speech transcripts

To store the text of captured thoughts so they can be routed to integrations or retrieved from the inbox if not acted upon immediately.

  • Retention: up to 10 years.
Capture metadata

To record the category, confidence score, routing destination, routing status, and timestamps associated with each captured thought, enabling inbox retrieval and routing logic.

  • Retention: up to 10 years.
Integration credentials (OAuth tokens)

To authenticate with third-party services such as Todoist on the user's behalf. Tokens are stored in the device Keychain and never leave the device.

  • Retention: up to 10 years.
  • Shared with: Todoist.
User preferences and settings

To remember preferred routing integrations per category, onboarding completion state, and other app configuration choices across sessions.

  • Retention: up to 10 years.
Subscription and purchase status

To verify whether the user holds an active Xura Pro entitlement and unlock Pro-tier integrations accordingly.

  • Retention: up to 10 years.
  • Shared with: Apple App Store (StoreKit).
Routing activity counter

To enforce the free-tier monthly routing limit of 25 captures and prompt upgrade when the limit is reached.

  • Retention: up to 31 days.

Your choices

You can remove the app from your device at any time, which stops any further data collection. Where applicable laws require it, you may also request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you; see the contact section below.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Additional notes

All transcription and classification runs on-device using Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer (requiresOnDeviceRecognition = true) and a bundled Core ML model. A captured thought is only transmitted off-device when the user explicitly approves routing to an external integration (e.g. Todoist REST API). Free-tier integrations (Apple Reminders, Apple Notes, Apple Messages, Things 3, Obsidian) use on-device system frameworks or local URL schemes and involve no data leaving the device. The monthly routing counter is stored locally via UserDefaults and is keyed by calendar month; it is not synced to any server.
